macOS Catalina 10.15

macOS Catalina (version 10.15) is the sixteenth and, as of August 2020, current major release of macOS, Apple Inc.'s desktop operating system for Macintosh computers. It is the successor to macOS Mojave and precedes macOS Big Sur, which is planned to be released in the second half of 2020. It was announced at WWDC 2019 on June 3, 2019, and was released to the public on October 7, 2019. Wikipedia
